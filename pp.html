Privacy policy

Types of Information Collected and Security

When orders are placed, we will ask for billing information to complete your purchase. All information collected through our shopping cart's checkout pages is through a 256 bit encrypted connection to your browser, using a valid SSL certificate. Your billing name, address, and purchase details are retained in our secure databases. We do not store or mail your credit card numbers. Credit card details are processed via a secure gateway. If you prefer, we have provided several alternate methods for online purchasing of our products and services: If using the PayPal service to checkout, all billing information is provided once you are passed off to their secure transaction servers at PayPal. They handle everything.

Disclosure of Information

The personal information you have provided us and the information about your order will not be shared or given away for any reason whatsoever outside of what is needed to process your order and complete your transaction. All personal information collected on our site is done via a 256 bit encrypted connection to your browser, under a verified SSL site certificate. You may check this from your browser.

The Sale of AlcoholicBeverages

As required by state and federal law, this website does not sell alcohol to persons under the age of 21. By using this site you swear and affirm that you are over the age of 21. Alcohol purchased from this site must be intended for personal consumption and not for resale. Please do not use this site if you do not agree with these terms. Misrepresentation of age, intentional or unintentional, in order to purchase alcohol by a minor or for a person under the age of 21 will result in prosecution to the fullest extent of the law.

If you have any questions or comments please contact us by phone (818) 244-1036 or https://www.glendaleliquor.com

Fraud

It is the intention of Glendaleliquor to prosecute credit card fraud to the fullest extent of all state, federal, and international laws. We will provide any and all transaction information, including (but not limited to) all trace information to the purchasing computer, to State, Federal and International Authorities, including the FBI, The Department of Justice, The Federal Trade Commission, and the National White Collar Crime Center.

Cookies:

When you visit the Glendaleliquor.com site, we collect basic information that does not identify individual users. Our site uses cookies to improve navigation analyze traffic, and keep track of products. These cookies do not contain any confidential or personal information.
