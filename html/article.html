Sunset Breeze

A refreshing mix of citrus vodka, pineapple juice, and a splash of grenadine

Description:

The Sunset Breeze is a refreshing and vibrant cocktail that perfectly captures the colors of a summer sunset. Combining the tangy flavors of cranberry and pineapple with a hint of orange, this drink is both sweet and slightly tart, making it an ideal choice for a relaxing evening or a festive gathering. The addition of vodka provides a smooth base that complements the fruit juices beautifully. Garnished with an orange slice and a cherry, the Sunset Breeze not only tastes delightful but also looks stunning.

Ingredients:
  • 2 oz vodka
  • 3 oz cranberry juice
  • 3 oz pineapple juice
  • 1 oz orange juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Orange slice and cherry for garnish (optional)
Instructions:
  • Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.
  • Pour in the vodka, cranberry juice, pineapple juice, and orange juice.
  • Shake well until the mixture is well chilled.
  • Fill a tall glass with ice cubes.
  • Strain the mixture from the shaker into the glass.
  • Garnish with an orange slice and a cherry, if desired.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy your Sunset Breeze!

This cocktail is perfect for any occasion, whether you are hosting a summer barbecue, enjoying a quiet evening at home, or celebrating with friends. Its bright colors and refreshing taste are sure to impress and delight.
